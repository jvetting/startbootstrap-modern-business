HOW?

Pests are good at hiding, and sometimes ordinary detective simply doesn't cut it. In these situations, we offer the services of our canine pest trackers, who have natural senses and instinct combined with the training necessary to get the job done. Dogs have been used on farms to hunt pests since humans began farming, and they've been helping humans hunt for the past 20,000 years.

WHY?

Using our team of rat dogs is cleaner than poison and more efficient than traps. Instead of leaving traps and waiting for the infestation to come to us, our dogs take us to the infestation. Using poison can get messy, traps take time to catch pests, and return visits are needed to clean up traps. Dogs find the rats and directly eliminate them without the slow process of trapping over time.

HUMANE?

Our dogs are trained to quickly kill rodents when they have been found. A quick death from our dogs is painless and more humane than a slow death from being poisoned in a trap. We know it's ugly business, so we aim to make it as humane as possible.
