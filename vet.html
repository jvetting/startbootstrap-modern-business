Our Commitment To Veterans


To learn more about our veteran discount, simply ask our staff about it while arragning an appointment over the phone or by email. Using our online form to arrange an appointment? Great! Let our staff know of your interest when they contact you by your preferred method to confirm your appointment.

GIVING BACK TO VETERANS

You've fought for us. Now let us fight for you.

Veteran Owned

Calvert Exterminators is veteran owned and determined to show support for the veteran community by offering veteran discounts to the brave men and women who have fought for our country. The owner and president of Calvert Exterminators, Dennis Heflin, has been giving back to the veteran community for years at the American Legion Gray Ray Post 220.

Veteran Discount

Eligible customers who provide proof of service may receive a military discount as a thank you for their service. Enquire with our staff about it while arranging an appointment.
