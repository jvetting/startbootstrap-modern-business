How do I arrange an appointment online?
Our online form is a convenient method of notifying us that you need service without having to explain all your contact information over the phone! By the time we contact you to set a date and time, we will already have that basic information from the online form! This saves time for everyone involved. To arrange an appointment online, click the large "Contact Us" button in the nav bar. Next, navigate to the "Arrange a service" section of the contact page. From there, fill out the entries with your contact information and reason for contact. Include any other important details in the "Additional notes" section, press the button to consent to our contact you, and press submit.
How does the Contact form work?
By filling out and submitting the online contact form, you are notifying us that you wish to contract service at the address you entered. Within 2 to 3 business days, we will follow up with you by your preferred contact method, phone or email, with an available date and time for service and any other necessary information. Reply with the time that works best and your appointment will be arranged!
How does the Feedback form work?
The Feedback form is a quick way to let us know whether we're doing everything right. If you like the website experience, simply rate your experience out of 5 stars and click "Send message". Feedback about the website does not need additional notes. However, if you would like to provide feedback about anything other than the website experience, please let us know so in the "Additional notes" section. All feedback is anonymous.
Do you collect any information?
We do not collect any information that you do not consent to providing us. We only use the information you agree to give us through the contact form to arrange your service. We do not use cookies or any other data collection.
