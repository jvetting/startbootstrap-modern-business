HOW ANIMAL CONTROL SERVICES WORK

From bats to copperheads, we'll trap and remove them.

THE STEPS:

Inspection
A Calvert specialist will perform a thorough examination of your property by examining typical nesting locations and other risk areas that may attract an animal problem. Calvert Exterminators utilizes tried-and-true detection methods along with a keen eye for even the most minute details in order to locate the animal, or animals, in question before trapping and removing them. Upon completing the initial sweep, we will recommend an ongoing service method along with a personalized price tag, just for you.
Our specialists will utilize the information given about the animal along, with typical behavior patterns and infestation signs, to locate it wherever it may lie. If Orkin finds a termite infestation or the potential for one, he will discuss the best solution for your situation.
Termidor® Liquid Treatment
The treatment offers immediate protection as well as protection from future infestations throughout the duration of the protection plan.
Dry Foam And OrkinFoam
OrkinFoam treatments expand to reach critical areas, providing extra coverage and protection against termites.
Sentricon® Bait and Monitoring
Sentricon® targets at-risk locations around your home such as, old tree stumps, moist soil, irrigation sprinkler heads, roof downspouts and other moist areas.
After the initial treatment, your Calvert Exterminator will establish a recurring protection plan to monitor and protect your property for any future problems. Our ongoing protection involves monthly visits, during which we ensure that the unlikely has not come to pass, and no new critters have invaded your home.

HOW MONTHLY ANIMAL CONTROL WORKS:

FIRST VISIT

One of our friendly technicians will treat the inside and outside of your home and develop a customized plan for protection.

REGULAR VISITS

Pests are always active, so you need protection to stay ahead of them. Our custom treatments adapt with the season to address seasonal pest activity in your area, giving you protection all year long. We'll come back to re-treat outside your home every season for a total of four treatments, including the initial service. And if pests come back, so will we - at no cost to you. It is all a part of our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee.
