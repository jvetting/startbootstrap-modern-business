American Cockroaches

By Gary Alpert - http://www.uos.harvard.edu/ehs/pes_american_cockroach.shtml, CC BY 2.5, Link

Pest Description

The American Cockroach is the largest home invading cockroach in Maryland, invading basements, sewers, and food storage areas. They are active mainly in warmer temperatures, but can also survive in cold temperatures. Once they have invaded a houses' food supply, they will spread disease through direct contact with food and other surfaces.

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Brown
  • 2 in. size, oval in shape

Diseases

  • Salmonellosis
  • Typhoid Fever
  • Cholera
  • Dysentery
  • More

German Cockroaches

By Lmbuga - Own work, CC BY-SA 3.0, Link

Pest Description

German Cockroaches are mainly found in small cracks close to food and water. Preferring damp and humid residential areas, they will invade the kitchens, pantries, and other food preparation areas of the homes in said residential areas. They are very common in American homes and are responsible for many illnesses spread by the surfaces they touch.

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Light brown or tan with two stripes on back
  • 1/2 to 5/8 in. size, oval in shape

DISEASES

  • Salmonellosis
  • Typhoid Fever
  • Cholera
  • Dysentery
  • More

Oriental Cockroaches

By Alvesgaspar - Self-photographed, CC BY-SA 4.0, Link

Pest Description

Oriental Cockroaches are mainly found in damp areas with decaying food, such as sewers, basements, firewood piles, and crawl spaces.

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Dark brown or black
  • 1 in. size, long in shape

DISEASES

  • Salmonellosis
  • Typhoid Fever
  • Cholera
  • Dysentery
  • More

Brown-banded Cockroaches

Public Domain, Link

Pest Description

Brown-banded Cockroaches fly short distances to reach high spots in the kitchens and bathrooms they are likely to reside in. They prefer warm, dry locations and tend to fly toward the ceiling, unlike most other roaches. They are easily distinguished by the banding along their body and wings along with their tendency to fly.

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Black
  • 16 in. size, long and oval in shape

DISEASES

  • Salmonellosis
  • Typhoid Fever
  • Cholera
  • Dysentery
  • More
