HOW PEST CONTROL SERVICES WORK

Ants, cockroaches, mosquitoes, hornets, mice, rats, and more...

THE STEPS:

Inspection

A Calvert specialist will perform a thorough examination of your home by checking your basement or crawlspace for moisture, your foundation for wood debris, and other risk areas that may attract an infestation. Calvert Exterminators utilizes tried-and-true detection methods along with a keen eye for even the most minute details in order to fully assess the level of infestation. Upon completing the initial sweep, we will recommend a service method along with a personalized price tag, just for you.

If Calvert Exterminators locates an ongoing or potential infestation, we will recommend the best solution for your situation.

Solutions

The solutions utilized by our exterminators varies based on the situation, putting the right amount of pressure on the infestation to bring the results with minimal cost to you! Our methods and technologies range from the most advanced pesticides to tried-and-true rodent traps. Like a mechanic choosing the right tool for the job, our solutions are selected based on analysis of the situation.

After the initial treatment, your Calvert Exterminator will establish a recurring protection plan to monitor and protect your property for any future infestations. Our ongoing protection involves monthly visits, during which we ensure that the unlikely has not come to pass, and no new infestations have begun to take root.
