HOW TERMITE CONTROL WORKS

Homeowners lose an average of $3,000 to termite damage. Stay ahead of this unseen enemy.

TREATMENTS:

Before directly infesting a home, termites live in the soil around your home or property and will emerge to eat away at the foundation. Subterranean colonies such as there are very large. By treating the soil itself, we hit the termites where they live. We treat the soil by digging a trench around the foundation, treating the soil, and refilling the trench. The refilled trench acts as a barrier, killing any termites that attempt to enter or exit the property.

If an infestation becomes severe enough, we will treat the wood itself inorder to cut off the colony's food supply. Termiticide spray can be sprayed directly onto or injected into the wood around the perimeter of the house and at entry points.

Termite bait can be used to slowly and reliably starve out termite colonies. By installing bait traps around the perimeter of the property and monitoring the traps on regular return visits, we can examine how well our placements are working. By examining the success of traps, we can adjust their locations maximum efficiency.
