HOW TERMITE CONTROL WORKS

Homeowners lose an average of $3,000 to termite damage. Stay ahead of this unseen enemy.

THE STEPS:

Inspection

A Calvert specialist will perform a thorough examination of your home by checking your basement or crawlspace for moisture, your foundation for wood debris, and other risk areas that may attract an infestation. Calvert Exterminators utilizes tried-and-true detection methods along with a keen eye for even the most minute details in order to fully assess the level of infestation and damage. Upon completing the initial sweep, we will recommend a service method along with a personalized price tag, just for you.

If Calvert Exterminators locates an ongoing or potential infestation, we will recommend the best solution for your situation. Our solutions vary based on the current season, species of termite, location of infestation, and extent of infestation. These factors are all important to solving your problem while saving you money.

After the initial treatment, your Calvert Exterminator will establish a recurring protection plan to monitor and protect your property for any future infestations. Our ongoing protection involves monthly visits, during which we ensure that the unlikely has not come to pass, and no new infestations have begun to take root.

HOW MONTHLY PEST CONTROL WORKS:

FIRST VISIT

One of our friendly technicians will treat the inside and outside of your home and develop a customized plan for protection.

REGULAR VISITS

Pests are always active, so you need protection to stay ahead of them. Our individualized treatments adjust with the season to protect your property from seasonal pest activity in your area.
